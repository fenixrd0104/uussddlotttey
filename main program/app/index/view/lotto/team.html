LOTTO is more than just a token - it 's an event

LOTTO is a culmination of the talents of our various team members and contributors. The idea for LOTTO started as something simple: an exciting event on the Ethereum blockchain that could change the lives of a few LOTTO token holders per week. It has evolved into something far more complex- an innovative ERC20 token with a unique lottery mechanism and a robust community supporting it. Our saavy team will be using their various talents to make LOTTO a major presence amongst Ethereum DeFi projects.
